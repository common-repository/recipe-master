Professional Recipe management and pricing for small businesses (chocolatiers, bakeries, cafes etc.)

== Description ==

= Features =
An overview of Recipe Master's features:

*   Securely store all your recipes on your Wordpress website
*   Two user types, Head Chefs can define recipes while chefs can view them
*   Sophisticed support for sub-recipes and composite recipes (meals, selection boxes etc)
*   Understand your Cost of Goods and pricing
*   Easily scale recipes
*   Organise your recipes by types and selections
*   Automatic generation of ingredient listings, allergen details and dietary notes
*   Integration into your Wordpress website making showing recipes or product details straightforward
*   Daily checklists for your EHO records
*   Secure storage for process documentation, handbooks etc.
*   Developed by a working food business so you know all features are road tested in a real business before being released

= WP Recipe Maker Premium =

Looking for some more advanced functionality? Recipe Master Premium adds the following features:

*   Price Calculator with support for retailer margins and markups, to show exactly how much profit you will make
*   Chocolate composition calculator to help blend chocolate types
*   Support for additional information on ingredient listings such as E number warnings

This plugin is in active development. Feel free to contact us with any feature requests or ideas.
